About Us

  • We are a company called Dazy Software. Our goal is to make living on Earth easy and interesting for absolutely everyone. We make various software for absolutely different types of activities from educational institutions to automotive manufacturing. We are currently developing a Linux-based operating system for visually impaired and also for children with disabilities. In the future we want to implement a messenger for visually impaired people, children with disabilities, older people and make the interface as customizable as possible for each person!

  • As of 2022, we are working remotely in over 60 countries. We've never had a personal office or cubicle, we do everything online. That's a plus, because we are independent of where we live and can work from anywhere in the world. For the most part, our employees are happy with our company and what they and our company do for the community. We will see absolutely everyone on our team who wants to do good and benefit the community.
